Testimonials

Pam

I am so grateful to Dr. Sheline for his healing guidance and support as I turned my life around in extraordinary ways. My weight has dropped from 310 to 155 lbs., a level I have maintained for over a year. I am no longer pre-diabetic, my cholesterol and blood pressure are now perfect, I no longer take antidepressant medication after depending on them for decades, and I have no more problems with arthritis in my knees (quite a surprise for this 67 year old woman). I move through my day enjoying my body — moving rapidly up and down stairs every day at work, dancing freely twice a week, doing yard work. I can’t remember a time when I felt stronger. People have told me that I seem to have the energy of a 25 year old!

One of the most important ways Dr. Sheline has helped me is to provide me resources to understand the science behind healthy nutrition, so that I am confident that what I am eating is the best I can do for maintaining my health and living a long life. I have made big changes in my diet, some of which I did not expect to enjoy. Yet, I have never enjoyed eating as much as I do now. I used to always feel that I needed to go hungry in order to maintain a healthy weight. But I can happily report that with my plant-based diet, I eat when I am hungry. No more hunger, no calorie counting.
